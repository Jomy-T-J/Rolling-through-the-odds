# Module 6: Monitoring, Evaluation & Reflection for Corporates

**Primary SDGs:** 4, 7, 10, 11, 12, 13

## Overview
Builds skills to monitor, evaluate, and reflect on sustainability action plans. Learners design M&E frameworks, collect/interpret data, reflect on outcomes, and connect to SDG-aligned reporting.

## Content Topics
- Monitoring vs Evaluation: monitoring (ongoing tracking) vs evaluation (periodic assessment)
- M&E logic chain: Inputs → Activities → Outputs → Outcomes → Impacts
- Indicators: clear, measurable, relevant, feasible (quantitative + qualitative)
- Baselines and targets: where we are now, where we want to be, by when
- Data sources: internal systems (energy meters, ERP, HR), external data, surveys/interviews
- Data collection methods: regular reporting, checklists, surveys, site visits, interviews
- Basic analysis: trend analysis, before vs after comparison, identifying outliers/patterns
- Limitations and uncertainties: data quality, missing data, attribution challenges
- Structured reflection: what worked/didn't work, what surprised us, what would we do differently
- Adaptive management: adjusting actions based on evidence
- SDG-aligned reporting: linking M&E results to SDG targets, communicating to different audiences
- Avoiding greenwashing: transparency about limitations, reporting successes and challenges

## Activities
1. **Design an M&E Framework for an Action Plan** — Use your action plan from Module 5. Create: intervention summary, logic chain (inputs→impacts), 3-6 indicators with name/definition/unit/baseline/target/data source, reporting plan (quarterly/annual)
2. **Data Interpretation & Mini Impact Report** — Write 500-700 words using real or hypothetical data: context, data summary (baseline vs actual vs target), interpretation (what data suggests, surprises), limitations (data quality, attribution), SDG links (at least 2)
3. **Reflection & Adaptive Management Note** — Write 400-600 words: what worked well, what didn't work/challenging, what surprised you, lessons learned, specific adjustments to action plan/indicators/targets, how to embed learning, what support you need

## Assessment
- Completeness and clarity of M&E framework (logical chain, indicators, baselines, targets)
- Quality of mini impact report (data use, interpretation, honesty about limitations, SDG alignment)
- Depth and practicality of reflection & adaptive management note (insight, learning, proposed adjustments)

## Reflection Prompt
- What was most challenging about designing or using an M&E framework?
- How has this module changed your understanding of monitoring, evaluation, and reflection?
- What is one M&E practice you will start using in your work?
- How do you see M&E and reflection supporting better sustainability outcomes in your organization?
